# Highlights

(*Nature Communications* does not require a separate Highlights
section, but a short bulleted summary is often requested by editors
during the initial review process. The five bullets below each fall
within the 85-character limit used by *Cell Press*–style highlights
and can be repurposed for a press release, a graphical-abstract
caption, or a one-line summary inside the cover letter.)

- First systematic per-pair discrimination map of all 528 layer
  pairs in a protein language model.
- 95,142 mutations across five DMS panels (CBS, GAL4, PABP, PTEN,
  TEM1) computed under ESM-2 (esm2_t33_650M_UR50D).
- Early–Late layer-pair interactions dominate the 528-pair landscape
  (T₃ z = 62 vs within-protein null; q = 10⁻³).
- Cross-protein recurrent layer pairs concentrate at depths 30–32;
  layers 18–27 contribute zero.
- PABP forms a singleton characterized by Early–Middle interactions;
  the other four proteins share the EL-dominated fingerprint.
